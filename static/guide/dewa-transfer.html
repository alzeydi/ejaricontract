How to move your DEWA
connection in Dubai

Updated June 2026 · ⏱ New connection within 24 hours · Deposit refund: 7–10 days

DEWA does not literally transfer an account between addresses in Dubai. To move your electricity and water, you close the account at your old property (Move Out) and open a new one at your new property (Move In). Both can be submitted on the same day via the DEWA app — your new connection is usually active within 24 hours.

i

Before you start

Make sure you already have a valid Ejari certificate for the new property and know the 9-digit Premises Number for the new address. Without these, the Move In application will fail.
If you have not yet registered Ejari for the new address, do that first — see our Ejari registration guide.
1

Submit Move Out for old property

Open the DEWA app or dewa.gov.ae, sign in, and select Move Out for your current address. Set the move-out date — DEWA reads the final meters automatically on that date and issues a final bill. 📱 DEWA app recommended
2

Pay the final bill

The final bill arrives by SMS and email within 24–48 hours of move-out. Pay any outstanding balance — DEWA will not release the security deposit refund until the bill is fully paid.
⚠️ If you skip this step, the deposit is held indefinitely and can affect future DEWA applications.
3

Submit Move In for new property

In the same DEWA app, select Move In. Enter the new property's Ejari registration number and 9-digit Premises Number. DEWA verifies the details against the DLD database and auto-fills your unit information.
4

Pay the new deposit and connection fee

You pay a fresh security deposit at the new property — the old deposit is refunded separately to your bank account.
Apartment depositAED 2,000
Villa depositAED 4,000
Connection fee (small meter)AED 100
Connection fee (large meter)AED 300
💡 You can submit Move Out and Move In on the same day so the new connection is ready before you physically move.
✓

New connection active within 24 hours

Once payment is confirmed, DEWA activates your new connection within 24 hours. You receive a confirmation SMS with the new account number. The old deposit is refunded to your bank account within 7–10 working days after the final bill is paid. ⚡ Usually same day
