About the tool

Ejari Helper auto-fills the official Dubai Land Department (DLD) tenancy contract form using AI to read your uploaded Emirates IDs and Title Deed. The output is a signed-ready PDF that goes through Ejari registration on the first try when used correctly. The service costs AED 15 — no account required, no recurring subscription.

For the step-by-step Ejari registration process after the contract is signed, see our Ejari registration guide. For renewals, see the Ejari renewal guide.
